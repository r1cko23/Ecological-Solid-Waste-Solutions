Who We Are

We are a registered corporation with expertise in ecologically-sound technologies specializing in compostable waste management with technical and managerial proficiency. The company was established in 2017, with the aim of helping government, businesses, and institutions in the responsible collection, handling, treatment, and disposal of their solid waste.

Our Mission

Provide modern technology in the proactive management of waste at the point of segregation up to recycling for economic and sustainable use that benefit communities, businesses, and institutions, while continuously creating better technologies compliant to standard targets in achieving a sustainable and zero waste lifestyle and the conservation of the environment.

Our Vision

To be a leading force in the solid waste management industry, inspiring and empowering others to embrace circular economy principles, while creating more ecologically-aware and resilient partnerships with local, national, and international clients in all sectors for collaborations towards sustainable development.

Our Core Values

Environmental Responsibility

Committed to sustainable practices and environmental protection in all our operations.

Innovation & Excellence

Continuously developing cutting-edge solutions and maintaining high standards.

Integrity & Transparency

Operating with honesty and openness in all our business dealings.
